Torta de Huevo

Description

This recipe will help you make some savory, easy-to-make tortas de huevo. Tortas de huevo are sandwiches made with telera bread and commonplace throughout Mexico.

Ingredients

  • 1 telera bread
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of butter
  • 2 tablespoons of mayo
  • 1 pickled jalapeno
  • 4 pickled carrots
  • 2 slices of queso fresco
  • 4 slices of tomato
  • 2 leafs of lettuce
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil

Directions

  1. Heat up skillet with medium-high flame and add vegetable oil.
  2. Crack your eggs and whisk them in a plate with your black pepper and salt.
  3. Heat up comal using medium-high flame and spread butter on it.
  4. Pour eggs on the skillet and cook until eggs are solid.
  5. Cut telera in half and place on comal; make sure you evenly toast both sides.
  6. Take teleras and spread mayo on them.
  7. Add eggs to your telera, put on toppings, and serve!
