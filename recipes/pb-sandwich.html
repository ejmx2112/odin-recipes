Peanut Butter Sandwich

Description

Peanut butter sandwiches are a classic for those looking for something light or with a need for an on-the-go snack. You can hardly go wrong with these!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of peanut butter
  • 2 slices of bread

Directions

  1. Put your slices into the toaster on preferrable toasting level.
  2. Grab your slices and spread peanut butter on each one.
  3. Put your slices together and enjoy!
